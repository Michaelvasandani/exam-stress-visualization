Understanding Stress Variability

Stress variability is measured using standard deviation, which indicates how much a student's physiological stress levels fluctuate during the exam. A higher value means more erratic stress responses, while a lower value suggests more consistent stress levels. High variability may indicate moments of panic or relief throughout the exam.
